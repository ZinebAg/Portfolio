About Me

Hi there, my name is Zineb Agnaou. I am a Master student in Applied Mathematics with a minor in Data Science at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne.

I have completed a Bachelor in Mathematics, with a focus on Statistics during my third year. It allowed to build strong analytical, problem solving and reasoning skills. For my master, I decided to combine Statistics and Data Science.

I am currently pursuing a six-months internship at AXA Group Operations in the Advanced AI/ML & Research team. My main focus is on Large Language Models.

Starting from March, I will be doing my Master Thesis as a research intern at the Idiap Institute in the Machine Learning Group. I will be working on the Generalisation of Machine learning models to distribution shifts over time.

In addition to my interest in data science, I have several experiences in the associative world from which I acquired a great know-how on organization and project management as well as numerous soft skills. I can show flexibility and independence. I am very curious and interested in many subjects and fields. For this reason, I have followed several autonomous trainings in parallel to my activities. Wildfires within the United States

A python and R project to visualise and study wildfires in the USA.

Brazilian Aeronautics Accidents

A python and R project to visualise and study airplane crashes in Brazil. We tried to answer the following questions What are the factors favoring a plane crash? What are the similarities between accidents? How to reduce the risks of an aviation disaster for the safety of passengers?

Covid-19 the United States

A python and R project to visualise and study COVID-19 in the USA. The purpose of this investigation is to give an answer to the following question: How is the number of deaths from COVID-19 in the U.S. evolving through time? What are the main risk factors of deaths from COVID-19? What are the most dangerous conditions? How did the mortality rate evolve through time? Does the geolocation of the state have an impact on the number of cases and the number of deaths from COVID-19?

Modern Regression Methods

The Food dilemma

A matlab project with the aim to answer the following two issues: How does the carbon footprint emitted by our food vary according to our nutrition choices? How do global carbon emissions vary if an entire community begins to change its eating habits?
